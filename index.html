Instructions:

You are collecting boxing gloves for survival, collect all the boxing gloves to win the game

Avoid collision with enemies at all cost or you will lose.

Use the following keys for movement: W for up, A for left, S for down, D for right.

Click Play button to Start the game.

Click Pause button to Pause the Game, click Resume to start the game again.

Click Restart button if you want to restart.

Click Quit button to Quit the game.
